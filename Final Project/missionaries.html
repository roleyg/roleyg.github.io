Full Time Missionaries

Here at Temple Inn and Suites we are happy to provide you with world class accomodations and services to those that are serving full time temple missions for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

Our room options for full time missionaries include:


King Suite

This is our King suite. It is our largest room available with 650 sq ft. It featers a king bed, large bathroom with jacuzzi tub, and a fully functioning kitchen.






Junior Suite

This is our Junior suite. It is similar to our King Suite but with a little less floor space as 500 sq ft. It features a queen bed, large bathroom with jacuzzi tub, and a fully functioning kitchen.
